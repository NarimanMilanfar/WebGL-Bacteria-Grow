Super Bug Zapper - User Guide

Objective

Kill all bacteria before two of them grow too large!

How to Play

  • Click on a bacteria to kill it instantly
  • Bacteria grow continuously from the edge of the white disk
  • 5 to 10 bacteria spawn in random positions with different colors

Scoring

  • Player gains: +1 point for each bacteria killed
  • Game gains: +0.5 points per second (delay penalty)
  • Game gains: +5 points when a bacteria reaches threshold size

Win/Lose Conditions

  • You WIN: Kill all bacteria before 2 reach threshold size
  • You LOSE: If 2 or more bacteria grow to threshold size

Tips

  • Click quickly - the game gains points while you wait!
  • Prioritize larger bacteria that are close to threshold
  • Watch for bacteria on opposite sides of the disk
